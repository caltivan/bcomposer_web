Remove user data

You can remove your account and all data from our servers just by removing your account inside the BComposer PRO App, Just follow the next steps:

1. Go to the settings menu option in the left corner of the main screen

2. Choose from the bottom the remove account option.

3. Confirm and remember the erased data can't be undone.
